Oxidative Stress & Antioxidant Defense

Intro:
 Oxidative stress is a major contributor to aging, chronic disease, and cellular damage. Genetic variants that influence antioxidant defense mechanisms and mitochondrial health can significantly impact your body's ability to manage oxidative stress and prevent cellular degeneration. These SNPs provide insights into how well your body is equipped to handle environmental stressors and how to optimize health.

SNPs:

  • SOD2 (rs4880) [Genotype Placeholder]
    Encodes mitochondrial superoxide dismutase, an enzyme that neutralizes reactive oxygen species (ROS) in mitochondria. Variants can reduce mitochondrial protection, contributing to oxidative damage and aging.
    Recommended Labs: SOD2 enzyme activity, oxidative stress markers
    Recommended Actions: Support mitochondrial health with antioxidants like vitamin C, vitamin E, selenium, and CoQ10. Focus on antioxidant-rich foods like berries, green tea, and dark chocolate.
  • CAT (rs1001179) [Genotype Placeholder]
    Encodes catalase, an enzyme that detoxifies hydrogen peroxide. Variants may reduce catalase activity, leading to increased oxidative stress and aging.
    Recommended Labs: Catalase activity, oxidative stress markers
    Recommended Actions: Increase antioxidant intake with foods like garlic, onions, and cruciferous vegetables. Consider supplements like vitamin E, selenium, and zinc to support detoxification.
  • GPX1 (rs1050450) & GPX4 (rs713041) [Genotype Placeholder]
    Glutathione peroxidase enzymes that protect against oxidative stress and lipid peroxidation. Variants may impair the body's ability to neutralize oxidative damage, contributing to cellular aging and disease.
    Recommended Labs: Glutathione levels, oxidative stress markers
    Recommended Actions: Supplement with glutathione, vitamin C, and selenium. Focus on a diet rich in antioxidants like berries, green tea, and leafy greens.
  • NQO1 (rs1800566) [Genotype Placeholder]
    Quinone reductase enzyme that neutralizes reactive quinones, protecting the body from oxidative damage. Variants may reduce the enzyme's activity, increasing susceptibility to oxidative stress.
    Recommended Labs: NQO1 enzyme activity, oxidative stress markers
    Recommended Actions: Support detoxification with antioxidant-rich foods, including berries, garlic, and green tea. Consider supplementation with CoQ10 and vitamin C to enhance protective activity.
  • NOS3 (rs1799983) [Genotype Placeholder]
    Nitric oxide synthase gene that regulates vascular tone and oxidative balance. Variants can impair nitric oxide production, which affects vascular health and oxidative stress management.
    Recommended Labs: Nitric oxide levels, vascular function markers
    Recommended Actions: Support nitric oxide production with antioxidants, beets, and leafy greens. Regular cardiovascular exercise and a diet high in polyphenols can also help improve nitric oxide synthesis.
